Picture Frame Job Buddy

This calculator uses the traditional Art + Margin industry standard:

  • Mat Border Measurement: Calculated from the edge of your picture to the frame rebate internal edge i.e. This is not inclusive of the mat/picture overlap
  • Overlap: This is the mat/picture overlap. This doesn't impact the size of the frame, only what is visible of the picture. It is not included in the Mat Border measurement
Mat Cutter's Cheat Sheet:
To cut the opening, set your mat-cutter guides to the Mat Opening Start values, calculated below. The calculations are:
  • Start X (Left): Margin Left + Overlap
  • Start Y (Top): Margin Top + Overlap
This ensures your "Visible Mat" is exactly what you expect after the overlap is applied.
Example: Art 200mm | Margin 50mm | Overlap 5mm
